LUWRAIN welcomed you!

If you hear these words, your audio hardware is working perfectly.

the space bar to skip it and continue loading. 

If the LUWRAIN environment is not appear in a couple of minutes, there
are likely some compatibility issues.  If this is the case, we would
greatly appreciate your feedback providing a technical report with all
necessary details, as it's described on our website luwrain.org. This
information will help us to fix these problems in future releases.
